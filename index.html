In PSU, We Evolve.

The Pangasinan State University Urdaneta City Campus IT Department geared its students toward complete beginners looking to take their first steps into Information Technology. It shows the ability of the IT department and is to change the way education meets in the future as they produce many competent and innovative students globally.

VISION

We shape and mold learners to become an agent in technical, professional, and entreprenuerial fields to adapt to the vast innovation setup. We produce applications and technologies by finding the community’s needs. We mobilize partnerships with the corporate sector and government to provide a long-term solution to the country’s development goals. We partner with other institutions to develop per-to-peer collaboration as part of the strenghtening BSIT program. We inspire learners to hone their skills, innovative for the common good, and support the deprived and marginalized by giving back to the community.

MISSION

The BSIT graduates are expected to become globally competent, innovative, and socially and ethically responsible computing professionals engaged in life-long learning endeavors. They are capable of contributing to the country’s national development goals.

Facilities

The PSU Urdaneta Information Technology Department is commited to providing the best available resources to enable students to develop their theoretical and practical skills. The BSIT Department has one networking labarotary and seven modern fully equipped computer laboratories with up-to-date hardware and software. All computers are networked and linked to high-speed, dedicated internet access.

SOCIETY OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY ENTHUSIASTS

Society of Information Technology Enthusiast(SITE) is a team of well-rounded people working together for the total development of each individual by pursuing excellence in academics as well as extra-curricular activities serving as a role model for BSIT students.They promote collaboration among faculty members and students. It is headed by a Governor and a pool of advisors.
